About Me

Born and raised in France, I am currently living in the Caribbean working as a Full-Stack Web Developer.
After a few years working in the market finance sector, I turned to more IT-orientated positions for a top-tier international bank which eventually decided me to learn coding and become a full-time web developer.
My past experiences as a business analyst and in projects management (Agile, SCRUM) provided me with a solid basis for the web development process.

I am currently working with Ruby and Ruby on Rails, but as a tech passionate always eager to expand my competences and knowledge, I am also learning React and Swift for iOS apps.
Being Full-Stack allows me to gain a broad range of skills all the way from back-end to front-end development. I value simple content structure, a clean design and well-thought-out animations/interactions (to bring some interactivity).
I have a strong focus on speed, performance and reliability with a preference for easy-to-use and intuitive UX/UI.
I really love each aspects of development process, to build things from scratch and enjoys bringing an idea to life in the browser.
